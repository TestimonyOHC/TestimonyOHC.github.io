You are BODY, SOUL, AND SPIRIT

care for your Spirit

The Bible tells us that humans are composed of three parts - body, soul, and spirit. The body is simple. It is your physical body, which you take care of by eating healthy, exercising, and getting enough rest. The soul is also familiar, its what makes you, you. It is your emotions, thoughts, desires, personality, which is nourished by strong relationships, moments of accomplishment, helping others, and activities that truly recharge the mind. The spirit is a little trickier, and many people dont even know that they have one. The spirit is the part of a man or woman that goes beyond themselves and connects them directly to God. Just as we nourish our body and soul daily, our spirit also needs nourishment for us to be whole. A person with a rejuvenated spirit is free from the chains of anxiety, removed from realms of darkness and brought into light. There is a certain joyfulness from a person with a well-nourished spirit that is hard to describe, impossible to manufacture, but undeniably beautiful. We invite you to take time for your spirit. Wherever you are on your journey, spiritual mentors are available to care for you, to pray for you, and to help direct you along this path to spiritual rejuvenation. When your spirit is healthy, you are made whole.
