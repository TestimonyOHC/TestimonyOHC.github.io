our mission

To make it known throughout the Ohio City area that Jesus Christ is Lord! ★ ★ ★Provide a healthy environment for believers to meet together and grow in their faith.★ ★ ★Help people who are seeking God to find God.

who we are

★ ★ ★ We are Christians from the Cleveland area, both the east and the west, sharing the collaborative mission of seeing Jesus magnified in the Ohio City neighborhood. This is a joint effort of the Church in Cleveland on the west side and Cleveland Heights Church on the east side. We are interested in getting connected with all people regardless of age, race, identity, nationality, culture, political views, or social status. ★ ★ ★
